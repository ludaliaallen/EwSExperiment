Can you detect deepfakes?

This study investigates if humans can reliably detect AI-generated speech. You will listen to short audio clips and answer questions about them. The experiment takes approximately 10 minutes.

Your participation is completely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without disadvantage. Your responses will be stored anonymously and used solely for research and educational purposes.

You may request the deletion of your data at any point before completing the experiment. After anonymisation, individual data can no longer be identified or removed.

By clicking "Continue", you confirm that you are participating voluntarily and consent to the use of your responses for research and educational purposes.





Use headphones!

Make sure that you are wearing your headphones and that you now hear the sample audio. Adjust the volume so that it is comfortable for you. When you are done, press continue.


You are about to hear 48 short sentences spoken by different speakers. Some of the sentences were spoken by real people, and some were cloned using an AI voice synthesizer. Your task is to decide if each sentence is real or AI-generated. Try not to overthink your decisions — just go with your gut feeling.

When you are ready, click "Continue" and we will start.
